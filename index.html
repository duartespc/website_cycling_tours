About Us

Chain Cycling Tours was born from a common passion of two friends.

You may ask why we choose “Chain Cycling Tours”. Well, for us the key part of the name is the “Chain”. In cycling language, we all know what it means but in common language can also mean a bond and that's precisely what our end game is.

Our goal is to share experiences and create bonds with people that have same passion for cycling like we do and at the same time show some of our beautiful Portugal.

Are you ready to come and move your chain with us?!?
